Match the Doggo

Welcome to Match the Doggo!

How to play:

Match the Doggo is a simple, user-friendly pairing memory game.

The aim of the game is to find all pairs of dogs. There are 6 pairs to find!

  1. click on a card to flip it over.
  2. click on another card.
  3. If the pair you flipped are identical, they will stay flipped and you get a point!
  4. If they are not identical, they will flip back over and you'll have to try again!
  5. Find all the pairs to win!
